Why It's Affordable

You're Paying for Our Time, Not Supplies

Our pricing model is simple — and refreshingly honest.

🍽️

You Supply the Food

You bring (or pre-pay for) your dog's own food. We don't charge a premium for supplies — it's your dog's food, it should be your cost at retail price. This keeps our rates genuinely low.

🦮

What You're Paying For

Our time, energy, and expertise. Walking your dog. Feeding them. Keeping them company, comfortable, and cared for. That's it. No hidden margin on food, beds, or toys.

🏠

No Overheads of a Kennel

We operate from a real home — not a commercial premises. We don't have a building full of kennels to maintain. Those savings go directly back to you in the form of lower prices.

Loyalty Discounts

The Longer You Stay, The Less You Pay

We reward commitment. Combining sessions — half-days, full days, and overnights — counts towards your total unit tally. The more units booked, the higher the percentage discount applied to your total.

Stay Duration Discount You Pay Notes
ℹ️ How it works

Every half-day and every overnight counts as one unit. A full day (morning + afternoon) is two units. An overnight is one unit. Simply add up the total number of units in your booking, and the corresponding discount tier is applied to your total bill automatically. The discount is calculated on the combined rack-rate — not per-unit.

⚖️
Fair-Wage Floor — 35% Maximum Discount

Our discounts are capped at 35% to ensure that we always earn a fair wage for our time. We're passionate about dogs, but we're also a real business with real people behind it. The floor is set so that even on the longest stays, we're compensated fairly for every hour of care we provide.
