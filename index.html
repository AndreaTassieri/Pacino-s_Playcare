Your Dog Gets Our Full Attention. Always.

Most kennels and dog-sitters take in several families' pets simultaneously. That means unfamiliar dogs, potential conflicts, and divided attention. We do things differently.

  • 🔒
    Strictly One Family at a Time

    We never host dogs from multiple households simultaneously. Your dog will never encounter an unfamiliar dog during their stay.

  • 🧡
    Perfect for Shy or Anxious Dogs

    Dogs with anxiety, reactivity, or social sensitivities thrive in our low-stimulation, stress-free environment.

  • 👀
    Undivided Care & Attention

    Because we only look after your dog(s), AJ can focus entirely on their needs — routines, meals, walks, and play all tailored specifically to them.

  • 🏡
    A Home, Not a Kennel

    Your dog stays in a warm, real home environment — not a cage or a compound. Comfort and familiarity are at the heart of everything we do.

About Us

Hi, I'm AJ — and I've been a dog person my whole life.

Growing up, I shared my home with three wonderful dogs of completely different breeds and sizes. That experience taught me how differently each dog communicates, plays, and needs comfort.

That lifelong bond is what inspired Pacino's Playcare. Along with my business partner, we've built a service rooted in genuine care — not corporate routine. We know the difference a calm, loving environment makes.

We keep our operation intentionally small — one family at a time — because we believe quality of care should never be diluted for the sake of profit. Your dog deserves the very best.
