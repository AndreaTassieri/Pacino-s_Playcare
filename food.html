The Process

How Food Pre-Pay Works

Simple, transparent, and hassle-free. Here's what happens from enquiry to bowl.

1

Tell Us What They Eat

Include your dog's food brand, variety, and pack size in your booking enquiry or contact form.

2

We Find the Retail Price

We look it up at standard retailers (Pets at Home, Zooplus, supermarkets) and confirm the cost with you.

3

You Confirm & Pay

You pay the food cost (at cost price) alongside your booking — no surprises on the day.

4

We Purchase & Prepare

We buy the food before your dog arrives so it's ready and familiar from day one of their stay.
